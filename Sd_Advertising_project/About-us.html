What is WhatsApp Marketing?

WhatsApp Marketing is essentially implementing the

power of WhatsApp to optimize brand visibility,

drive organic traffic, and boost conversions.

By leveraging WhatsApp's 2.4 Billion active user

base and viral potential, businesses can enhance

their online presence, generate qualified leads,

and establish valuable customer connections.
